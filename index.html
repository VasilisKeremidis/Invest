About micro-credentials

Micro-credentials are short, focused learning programs designed to provide specific skills or knowledge in a particular area. Unlike traditional degree programs, which may take several years to complete and cover a broad range of subjects, micro-credentials are typically more targeted and can often be completed in a matter of weeks or months. They are often used for professional development, reskilling, or upskilling, allowing individuals to quickly gain competencies that are directly applicable to their careers.
